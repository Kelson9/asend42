AND MEDICAL STUDENTS

THE ASSOCIATION OF ENGINEERING AND MEDICAL STUDENTS(ASEND) is an organization comprising of Engineers,Doctors,Teachers and students who seek to be a solution to the nations problems.We are a family.It was established in 2014 by Engineer David Baar and Dr.Cabrel Ngongang with the initial goal of preparing high school students for concours in Cameroon.Over course of time, ASEND has become an organization which does not solelely prepare students for competitive entrance examinations bt also having a contribution to the emergence of Cameroon by 2035.

OUR IMPACT


We have impacted the lives of youths throughout Cameroon.In 2020,we succeeded in sending over 85 out of 150 students in their dream professional schools in Cameroon in the following fields: Medecine(Buea,Yaounde,Bamenda,Douala). Engineering (Polytechnic,FET,COT,EGEM,FASA etc). Agriculture(FASA,FAVM). Veterinary Medecine(Ngaoundere,FAVM-UB). We have an objective of leading 1000 students to their dream university in and out of the country in the above mentioned fields.


Each year,in accordance with the UN Sustainable Development goals, we celebrate the World Engineering Day on the 4th of March with students all over Cameroon to recognise the immense contribution of Engineering and Engineers in ASEND,Cameroon and the World .With developing and building on leadership and professionalism in the life of all members,engineers inclusive being a goal of ASEND, we see this day as one to create impact in the lives of the students.


We make sure that our team members gain professional skills in the course of carrying out ASEND activities. .With this, we impact skills and professionalism by giving them the opportunity to discover their hidden potentials.We do this through workshop. We train them in aspects like Public speaking,Self-discovery,Leadership and many more. We also go as far as organizing tech trainings for interested team members who aspire to gain some skillsets in the field of IT.
